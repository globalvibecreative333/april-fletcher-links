# Gmail Filter Setup — wvm2008 + aliases

**Goal:** Stop the noise without losing critical bills, account alerts, or things you want to read on your own time.

---

## How to add each filter (do this once per rule below)

1. In Gmail, click the **gear icon** (top right) → **See all settings**
2. Click the **Filters and Blocked Addresses** tab
3. Click **Create a new filter** (at the bottom)
4. A search box opens. Paste the **"From" field** content from the rule below into the **From** field
5. Click **Create filter** (bottom right of that box)
6. On the next screen, check the boxes listed under **"Actions"** for that rule
7. **Important:** Also check **"Also apply filter to N matching conversations"** — this retroactively cleans up your existing inbox
8. Click **Create filter**

You'll do this 3 times total. ~10 minutes.
