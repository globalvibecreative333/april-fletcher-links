## Layout + Visual Structure Notes

**Single column. Mobile-first. No sidebars.**

- **Max width:** 640–720px for text. Never full-width paragraphs.
- **Font:** One serif or one sans. Not both. 18px body minimum.
- **Spacing:** Generous vertical rhythm. 48–64px between sections. 24px between paragraphs.
- **Horizontal rules** (or whitespace) separate each section. Reader should feel where one ends and the next begins.

**Section order on the page:**

1. Headline + subheadline + primary CTA button *(above the fold — no hero image)*
2. The Problem
3. What This Is / What This Is Not *(stacked; "Is Not" as a short bullet list under the main block)*
4. What You Get *(bulleted, bold the deliverable name, emphasize the 48-hour and 1–1.5 page specifics)*
5. Who This Is For / Not For *(two columns on desktop, stacked on mobile)*
6. How It Works *(numbered, one line each + brief detail)*
7. Pricing + CTA *(centered, boxed, one button)*
8. FAQ *(plain Q&A, not collapsed — list is short enough to read in full)*
9. Final CTA *(repeat of the button)*

**CTA:**
- Same button text every time: **Start Your Audit**
- Links to a single-step checkout (Stripe Payment Link, Gumroad, or equivalent)
- No form. No "learn more." No newsletter capture.

**What to leave off:**
- No hero image or illustration
- No testimonials until you have real ones
- No founder photo above the fold
- No logos bar, no "featured in"
- No video

The page should feel like a well-written memo, not a marketing site. That matches the offer.
